Our Commitment to Child Safety

EchoStream is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We do not tolerate sexual exploitation, abuse, or endangerment of children in any form.

Prohibited Content

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Grooming, predatory behavior, or solicitation of minors
  • Harassment, threats, or exploitation of children

Detection & Enforcement

  • User reporting tools for inappropriate content
  • Moderator and admin review systems
  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Account suspension or permanent bans

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report content directly within the app using the report feature. Reports related to child safety are reviewed with priority.
